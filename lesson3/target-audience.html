Target Audience:

Persona 1:

Male. 30-40 years of age. Middle Class. 50k+ a year salary. Ethnicity: unimportant. Has a job working 40 hours a week. Has a wife and 1-3 kids. Enjoys sports, outdoor activities, and video games.

Persona 2:

Male. Early 20's. Middle Class upbringing. Full time student. Ethnicity: unimportant. Goes to school full time but is more into outdoor activities. Is constantly hiking, fishing, or doing watersport or snow sports.

Scenarios

What will be the weather at a specific time for my sons soccer game?

Will the sky be overcast today and effect fishing?

Will it be warm enough to go to the lake?

Will the weather dictate I leave for work early to avoid traffic?

What will the road conditions be like in the morning?
