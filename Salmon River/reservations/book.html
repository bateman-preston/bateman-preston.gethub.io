Our Adventurer Trip is fun for the whole family. Enjoy a safe and fun filled white water rafting experience that is guaranteed to provide thrills for the whole family. Our trips are all inclusive and you can look forward to enjoying a continental breakfast, followed by a 2 hour long rafting excursion experiencing the best of Idahos white water. Afterwards, we provide a cookout meal of hamburgers and hotdogs, chips, and drinks.


The Outdoorsman Trip provides the best experience for those wishing to connect with their inner mountain man. During this trip you will enjoy such activities as rafting, fishing, shotgun shooting, and hatchet throwing. Our trained staff will be there to give expert advice, show you the best fishing spots, and make sure you have a great time both on and off the river. You must be at least 16 years old and minors must be accompanied by an adult.


Warning: The Extreme Adventure Trip is not for the casual rafter. On this 4 hour adventure you will get to experience some of the largest and most technical rapids that Idaho has to offer. During this trip you encounter several class 4 rapids and even a small waterfall. All participants must be at least 18 years old and in good health and shape.
