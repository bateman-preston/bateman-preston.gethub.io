Adventurer Trip

Adventurer Trip Includes:

  • All Safety Equipment
  • 4 Hours of Rafting Time
    (depending on speed of river)
  • Expert River Guide
  • Continental Breakfast
  • Lunch Cookout
    (Hamburgers/Hotdogs)
  • Transportation To/From River

Outdoorsman Trip

Outdoorsman Trip Includes:

  • All Safety Equipment
  • 6 Hour Trip
    (3 hours on the river)
  • Expert River Guide
  • Continental Breakfast
  • All Fishing Rods/Tackle and Other Gear
  • Hatchet/Knife Throwing
  • Fly Fishing Instruction
  • Fish Cleaning/Cooking
    (Fish caught can be eaten at lunch cookout)
  • Shotgun Shooting
    (All gear provided)
  • Lunch Cookout
    (Hamburgers/Hotdogs)

Extreme Adventurer

Outdoorsman Trip Includes:

  • All Safety Equipment
  • 4 Hour Trip
  • Expert River Guide
  • Rafting over many class 3/4 class rapids
  • Rafting over a waterfall
  • Special Guidance
  • Continental Breakfast
  • Lunch Cookout
    (Hamburgers/Hotdogs)
